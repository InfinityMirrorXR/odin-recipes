Supreme Pizza

Description

A recipe on how to make a scrumptious supreme pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 sheet Lebanese bread
  • ½ cup ham diced
  • ⅓ cup onion halved then thinly sliced
  • ⅓ cup capsicum diced
  • ⅓ cup celery diced
  • ⅓ cup pineapple sliced (optional)
  • ½ cup mushrooms sliced
  • ½ cup (50g) tasty cheese freshly grated

Steps

  1. Spread tomato paste evenly over flat bread.
  2. Arrange all the toppings (except cheese) evenly over base.
  3. Sprinkle cheese over pizza. Bake for 15-20 minutes until cheese has melted and is golden brown. Cut into 6 pieces and serve with salad.
