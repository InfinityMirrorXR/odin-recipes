Meat Lovers Pizza

Description

A recipe on how to make a meat lovers pizza with all your favourite mouth watering toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 portion pizza dough
  • 2 tbsp barbecue sauce
  • 1 tbsp pizza sauce
  • 1 pepperoni stick, sliced
  • 1 chorizo sausage, sliced
  • 100g beef mince, cooked
  • 50g ham, diced
  • 1 tbsp mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C. Roll out pizza dough and place on hot pizza stone or baking tray.
  2. Pour the barbecue sauce and pizza sauce onto the dough and use the back of a dessert spoon to combine the two sauces and spread evenly over the dough.
  3. Spread the cooked mince over the dough then place the pepperoni, sausage and ham on top.
  4. Scatter the cheese evenly across the pizza. Bake in the oven for 20-25 mins or until the cheese is golden and melted.
